Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Do I get to choose my shades?
The starter set comes with our 3 best-selling shades: Raspberry Kiss (warm berry), Hazelnut (caramel neutral), and Fallen Leaves (cool plum). They're chosen because they look universally flattering on every skin tone — so you can't pick wrong.
What exactly is Multi-Colour Cream?
Think of it as your whole makeup bag in one tiny tin. It's a creamy, blendable balm you can swipe on your lips, dab on your cheeks, and tap onto your eyelids. One product, whole face, 30 seconds.
Are the ingredients really 100% natural?
Yep. Every ingredient is EWG-rated safe. We use real fruit and plant pigments instead of synthetic dyes, jojoba and olive squalane instead of petroleum, and shea butter and hyaluronic acid instead of fillers. Zero parabens, PEGs, fragrance, or talc.
Is it safe for sensitive skin?
Yes — our formula is non-comedogenic, fragrance-free, and designed for reactive skin. Customers with eczema, acne, rosacea, and pregnancy have written us thank-you emails. If you're extra reactive, patch-test first.
How long does the colour last?
4-6 hours of wear, depending on your skin type. The formula has a subtle stain effect, so even after the cream fades the pigment lingers naturally.
